FAQ

  • Why is supplementing needed?

    Most adjusters are honest and ethical, however most of them have never been in the industry or worked as contractors, therefore most of the time they miss line items that are crucial to complete the work. The burden of proof falls on the contractor to prove why additional line items are needed to complete the work. Therefore it is crucial to have someone with experience by your side.

  • What is the average claim increase?

    Increase in a claim varies greatly on different factors, from type of material being used, insurance company, to the state in which the work is being performed. However our average increase in a claim is about 15-30% of the initial claim value.

  • What is the average turnaround time on files?

    The average turnaround time on files is about 14 days from submittal. The insurance supplementing process is a give and take a negotiation process. Some of our clients prefer to close out files quickly and some prefer for us to take our time on files and push for as much of an increase as we can. We tailor to your company's needs!

  • How do we accommodate different companies?

    At Viewpoint Claim Solutions we integrate into different companies CRM systems like Jobnimbus and Salesforce to keep you updated on each step of the claims process.

  • When do I start supplementing a job?

    We always advise clients to start supplementing a job before the work starts to make sure we get as much as the supplementing approved as we can before work is started. Once work is completed and production pictures are available we do our final supplement for things that we might have missed.
